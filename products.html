Bombus

The Bombus compressor is a high-quality analogue-modelled mastering compressor based on the Buzz Audio DBC-M. Its diode bridge design gives it an amazing crunch to it, but is much cleaner than other classic compressors of the same type.


Mustela

Mustela is a FREE (yes free!) PWM compressor. PWM is a very clean design.

Honeycomb

The Honeycomb is modelled on Buzz Audio's renowned REQ2.2M mastering EQ which is found in many mastering studios around the world.


Bombyx

The Bombyx is a mastering EQ designed to be super smooth and transparent. With a 3dB/octave shelf, you can boost the top end without making anything harsh.
